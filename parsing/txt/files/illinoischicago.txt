Applying

When applying to University of Illinois--Chicago, it's important to note the application deadline is Jan. 15. The application fee at University of Illinois--Chicago is $50. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Feb. 1. It is selective, with an acceptance rate of 77 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
