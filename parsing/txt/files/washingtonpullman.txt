Applying

When applying to Washington State University, it's important to note the application deadline is rolling. The application fee at Washington State University is $50. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due N/A. It is selective, with an acceptance rate of 80 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
