Applying

When applying to University of Kansas, it's important to note the application deadline is rolling. The application fee at University of Kansas is $30. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due May 1. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 93 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
