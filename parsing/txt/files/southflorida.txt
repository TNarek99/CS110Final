Applying

When applying to University of South Florida, it's important to note the application deadline is March 1. The application fee at University of South Florida is $30. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due April 15. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 45 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
