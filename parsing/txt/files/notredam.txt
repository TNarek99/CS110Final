Applying

When applying to University of Notre Dame, it's important to note the application deadline is Jan. 1, and the early action deadline is Nov. 1. The application fee at University of Notre Dame is $75. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due March 1. It is most selective, with an acceptance rate of 20 percent and an early acceptance rate of 38.9 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
