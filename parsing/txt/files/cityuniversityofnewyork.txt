Applying

When applying to CUNY--New York City College of Technology, it's important to note the application deadline is Feb. 1. The application fee at CUNY--New York City College of Technology is $65. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Aug. 1. It is least selective, with an acceptance rate of 74 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
