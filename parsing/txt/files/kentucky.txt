Applying

When applying to University of Kentucky, it's important to note the application deadline is rolling, and the early action deadline is Dec. 1. The application fee at University of Kentucky is $50. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Aug. 1. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 91 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
