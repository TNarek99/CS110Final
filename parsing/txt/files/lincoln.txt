Applying

When applying to University of Massachusetts--Amherst, it's important to note the application deadline is Jan. 15, and the early action deadline is Nov. 1. The application fee at University of Massachusetts--Amherst is $75. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Feb. 1. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 58 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
