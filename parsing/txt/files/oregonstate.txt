Applying

When applying to Oregon State University, it's important to note the application deadline is Sept. 1, and the early action deadline is Nov. 1. The application fee at Oregon State University is $60. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Sept. 1. It is selective, with an acceptance rate of 78 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
