Applying

When applying to Colorado State University, it's important to note the application deadline is Feb. 1, and the early action deadline is Dec. 1. The application fee at Colorado State University is $50. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due May 1. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 81 percent and an early acceptance rate of 99.9 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
