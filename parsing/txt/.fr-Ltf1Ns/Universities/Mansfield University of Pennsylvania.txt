Applying

When applying to Mansfield University of Pennsylvania, it's important to note the application deadline is rolling. The application fee at Mansfield University of Pennsylvania is $25. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due N/A. It is less selective, with an acceptance rate of 86 percent.
