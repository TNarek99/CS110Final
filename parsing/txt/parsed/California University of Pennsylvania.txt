Applying

When applying to California University of Pennsylvania, it's important to note the application deadline is Aug. 22. The application fee at California University of Pennsylvania is $25. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Aug. 1. It is less selective, with an acceptance rate of 83 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
