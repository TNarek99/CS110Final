Applying

When applying to University of California--Santa Cruz, it's important to note the application deadline is Nov. 30. The application fee at University of California--Santa Cruz is $70. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Jan. 10. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 51 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
