Applying

When applying to Purdue University--West Lafayette, it's important to note the application deadline is rolling, and the early action deadline is Nov. 1. The application fee at Purdue University--West Lafayette is $60. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Feb. 1. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 59 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
