Applying

When applying to University of Arizona, it's important to note the application deadline is May 1. The application fee at University of Arizona is $50. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due May 1. It is selective, with an acceptance rate of 76 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
