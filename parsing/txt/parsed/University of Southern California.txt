Applying

When applying to University of Southern California, it's important to note the application deadline is Jan. 15. The application fee at University of Southern California is $80. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Feb. 15. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 18 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
