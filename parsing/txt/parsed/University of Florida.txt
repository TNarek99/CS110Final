Applying

When applying to University of Florida, it's important to note the application deadline is Nov. 1. The application fee at University of Florida is $30. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Dec. 31. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 48 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
