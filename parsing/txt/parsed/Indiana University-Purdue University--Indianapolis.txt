Applying

When applying to Indiana University-Purdue University--Indianapolis, it's important to note the application deadline is May 1. The application fee at Indiana University-Purdue University--Indianapolis is $55. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due May 1. It is selective, with an acceptance rate of 70 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
