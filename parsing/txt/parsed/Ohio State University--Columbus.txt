Applying

When applying to Ohio State University--Columbus, it's important to note the application deadline is Feb. 1, and the early action deadline is Nov. 15. The application fee at Ohio State University--Columbus is $60. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Feb. 1. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 49 percent and an early acceptance rate of 66.1 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
