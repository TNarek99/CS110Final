Applying

When applying to University of Illinois--Urbana-Champaign, it's important to note the application deadline is Dec. 1. The application fee at University of Illinois--Urbana-Champaign is $50. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Dec. 12. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 66 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
