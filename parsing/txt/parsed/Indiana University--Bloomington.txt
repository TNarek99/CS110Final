Applying

When applying to Indiana University--Bloomington, it's important to note the application deadline is rolling. The application fee at Indiana University--Bloomington is $60. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due April 1. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 78 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
