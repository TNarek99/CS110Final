Applying

When applying to University of Chicago, it's important to note the application deadline is Jan. 1, and the early action deadline is Nov. 1. The application fee at University of Chicago is $75. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due N/A. It is most selective, with an acceptance rate of 8 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
