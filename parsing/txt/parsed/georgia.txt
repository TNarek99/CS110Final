Applying

When applying to University of Georgia, it's important to note the application deadline is Jan. 15, and the early action deadline is Oct. 15. The application fee at University of Georgia is $60. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Jan. 24. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 53 percent and an early acceptance rate of 65.8 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
