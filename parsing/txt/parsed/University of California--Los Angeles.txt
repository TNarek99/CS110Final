Applying

When applying to University of California--Los Angeles, it's important to note the application deadline is Nov. 30. The application fee at University of California--Los Angeles is $70. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Jan. 15. It is most selective, with an acceptance rate of 17 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
