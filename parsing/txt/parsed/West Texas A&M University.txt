Applying

When applying to West Texas A&M University, it's important to note the application deadline is rolling. The application fee at West Texas A&M University is $40. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Aug. 15. It is selective, with an acceptance rate of 67 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
