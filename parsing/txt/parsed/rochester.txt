Applying

When applying to University of Rochester, it's important to note the application deadline is Jan. 5, and the early decision deadline is Nov. 1. The application fee at University of Rochester is $50. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Jan. 5. It is most selective, with an acceptance rate of 34 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
