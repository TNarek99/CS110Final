Applying

When applying to Indiana University-Purdue University--Fort Wayne, it's important to note the application deadline is Aug. 1. The application fee at Indiana University-Purdue University--Fort Wayne is $50. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Aug. 1. It is less selective, with an acceptance rate of 92 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
