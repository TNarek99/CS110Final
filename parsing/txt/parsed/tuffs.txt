Applying

When applying to Tufts University, it's important to note the application deadline is Jan. 1, and the early decision deadline is Nov. 1. The application fee at Tufts University is $70. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Feb. 1. It is most selective, with an acceptance rate of 16 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
