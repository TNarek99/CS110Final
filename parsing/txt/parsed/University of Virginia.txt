Applying

When applying to University of Virginia, it's important to note the application deadline is Jan. 1, and the early action deadline is Nov. 1. The application fee at University of Virginia is $70. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Jan. 1. It is most selective, with an acceptance rate of 30 percent and an early acceptance rate of 30.2 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
