Applying

When applying to Michigan State University, it's important to note the application deadline is rolling, and the early action deadline is Oct. 17. The application fee at Michigan State University is $50. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due N/A. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 66 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
