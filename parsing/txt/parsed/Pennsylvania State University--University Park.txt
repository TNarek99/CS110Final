Applying

When applying to Pennsylvania State University--University Park, it's important to note the application deadline is rolling. The application fee at Pennsylvania State University--University Park is $50. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due N/A. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 51 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
