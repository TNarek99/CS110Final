Applying

When applying to Virginia Tech, it's important to note the application deadline is Jan. 15, and the early decision deadline is Nov. 1. The application fee at Virginia Tech is $60. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Jan. 28. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 73 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
