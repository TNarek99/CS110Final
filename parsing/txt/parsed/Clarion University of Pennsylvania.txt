Applying

When applying to Clarion University of Pennsylvania, it's important to note the application deadline is rolling. The application fee at Clarion University of Pennsylvania is $40. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Aug. 15. It is less selective, with an acceptance rate of 96 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
