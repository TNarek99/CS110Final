Applying

When applying to Bloomsburg University of Pennsylvania, it's important to note the application deadline is rolling. The application fee at Bloomsburg University of Pennsylvania is $35. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Dec. 30. It is less selective, with an acceptance rate of 88 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
