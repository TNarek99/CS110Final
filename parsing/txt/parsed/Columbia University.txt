Applying

When applying to Columbia University, it's important to note the application deadline is Jan. 1, and the early decision deadline is Nov. 1. The application fee at Columbia University is $85. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Feb. 1. It is most selective, with an acceptance rate of 6 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
