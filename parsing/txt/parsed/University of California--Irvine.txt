Applying

When applying to University of California--Irvine, it's important to note the application deadline is Nov. 30. The application fee at University of California--Irvine is $70. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Dec. 31. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 39 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
