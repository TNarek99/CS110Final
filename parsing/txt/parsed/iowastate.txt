Applying

When applying to Iowa State University, it's important to note the application deadline is rolling. The application fee at Iowa State University is $40. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due July 15. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 87 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
