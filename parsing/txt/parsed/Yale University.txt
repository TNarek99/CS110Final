Applying

When applying to Yale University, it's important to note the application deadline is Jan. 1, and the early action deadline is Nov. 1. The application fee at Yale University is $80. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due March 1. It is most selective, with an acceptance rate of 7 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
