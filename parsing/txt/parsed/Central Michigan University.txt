Applying

When applying to Central Michigan University, it's important to note the application deadline is July 1. The application fee at Central Michigan University is $35. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due July 1. It is selective, with an acceptance rate of 69 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
