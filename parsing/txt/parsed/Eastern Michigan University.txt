Applying

When applying to Eastern Michigan University, it's important to note the application deadline is rolling. The application fee at Eastern Michigan University is $35. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due N/A. It is selective, with an acceptance rate of 75 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
