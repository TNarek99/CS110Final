Applying

When applying to University at Buffalo--SUNY, it's important to note the application deadline is rolling, and the early action deadline is Nov. 15. The application fee at University at Buffalo--SUNY is $50. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due N/A. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 60 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
