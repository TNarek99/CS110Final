Applying

When applying to Carnegie Mellon University, it's important to note the application deadline is Jan. 1, and the early decision deadline is Nov. 1. The application fee at Carnegie Mellon University is $75. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Dec. 1. It is most selective, with an acceptance rate of 24 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
