Applying

When applying to Boston University, it's important to note the application deadline is Jan. 3, and the early decision deadline is Nov. 1. The application fee at Boston University is $80. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Jan. 3. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 33 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
