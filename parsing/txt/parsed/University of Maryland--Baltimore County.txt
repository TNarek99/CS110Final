Applying

When applying to University of Maryland--Baltimore County, it's important to note the application deadline is Feb. 1, and the early action deadline is Nov. 1. The application fee at University of Maryland--Baltimore County is $50. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Feb. 1. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 59 percent and an early acceptance rate of 51.8 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
