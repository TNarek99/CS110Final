Applying

When applying to Georgia Institute of Technology, it's important to note the application deadline is Jan. 10, and the early action deadline is Oct. 15. The application fee at Georgia Institute of Technology is $75. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Dec. 1. It is most selective, with an acceptance rate of 32 percent and an early acceptance rate of 45.5 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
