Applying

When applying to University of Utah, it's important to note the application deadline is April 1, and the early action deadline is Dec. 1. The application fee at University of Utah is $45. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due March 31. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 81 percent and an early acceptance rate of 87.4 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
