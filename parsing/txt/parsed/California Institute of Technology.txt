Applying

When applying to California Institute of Technology, it's important to note the application deadline is Jan. 3, and the early action deadline is Nov. 1. The application fee at California Institute of Technology is $75. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Dec. 31. It is most selective, with an acceptance rate of 9 percent and an early acceptance rate of 13.6 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
