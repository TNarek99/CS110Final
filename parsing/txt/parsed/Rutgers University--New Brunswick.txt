Applying

When applying to Rutgers University--New Brunswick, it's important to note the application deadline is rolling. The application fee at Rutgers University--New Brunswick is $65. Scores for either the ACT or SAT test are due Dec. 1. It is more selective, with an acceptance rate of 58 percent.

For more information about the tests, essays, interviews and admissions process, visit the Applying to College knowledge center.
